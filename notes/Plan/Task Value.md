### Habits and Dailies

$\text{Task Value}=\frac{2.5}{1+4e^{0.1x}}+0.5$
Where $x$ is the Task Counter
One-directional habits have their value halved at Cron until it reaches 0.1, when it's set to 0

### To Do's

$\text{Task Value}=-\frac{50x}{x+30}$  
Where $x$ is the number of days since To Do creation
