    - Costs 15 MP
    - XP $= 2.5\sqrt{\text{INT}}$

    - Costs 25 MP

- Level 35
    - Streak freeze
    - Costs 50 MP
    - Damage is still taken
- Level 50
    - Stat buffs (4 separate skills) based on each stat
